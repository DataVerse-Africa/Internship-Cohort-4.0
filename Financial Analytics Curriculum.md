**Duration:** 12 Weeks (3 Months)  
**Domain:** Financial Analytics  
**Track Level:** Intermediate

## üéØ **Finance-Specific Learning Objectives**

By the end of this track, interns will be able to:
- Analyze financial statements and performance metrics
- Build financial dashboards for P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow
- Perform credit risk analysis and customer segmentation
- Detect fraud patterns using data analytics
- Conduct profitability and cost analysis
- Analyze investment portfolios and market trends
- Build financial forecasting and budgeting models
- Understand regulatory compliance requirements (KYC, AML)
- Create pricing and revenue optimization models
- Communicate financial insights to executives and stakeholders

#### **Week 1: Finance Analytics Foundations**

**Learning Outcomes:**
- Understand financial data ecosystem and reporting standards
- Master financial terminology and key ratios
- Learn accounting fundamentals for analysts
- Navigate financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)

**Key Topics:**
- Finance analytics career landscape
- Financial reporting standards (IFRS, GAAP basics)
- Double-entry bookkeeping principles
- The three financial statements and their relationships
- Chart of Accounts structure
- Financial periods and reporting cycles
- Data sources in finance (ERP, banking systems, payment gateways)
- Financial data quality and reconciliation

**Hands-On Activities:**
- Case study: Reading and interpreting real company financial statements
- Exercise: Linking P&L, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow
- Exercise: Building a simple accounting journal

**Deliverable:** 
- **Mini Project 1:** "Financial Statement Deep Dive Analysis"

  - Calculate 15+ key financial ratios
  - 3-page report on company financial health
  - Identify trends and red flags

**Tools:** Excel, Google Sheets

**Learning Outcomes:**

- Build revenue dashboards

**Key Topics:**

- Gross margin vs. net margin analysis
- Contribution margin analysis
- Break-even analysis
- Revenue growth rate calculations
- Seasonal revenue patterns

**SQL Focus:**
